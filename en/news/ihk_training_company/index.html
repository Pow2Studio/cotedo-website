02/09/2018

Our apprentice has got her certificate in her pocket

In February 2018, Angela Burgmair has finished her vocational training at cotedo. We would like to congratulate our newly minted „Kauffrau für Büromanagement“ (office management assistant) on her successful graduation. cotedo as her training company also scores high marks.

Director Stefan Gatt explains his motivation: “Over the last ten years, cotedo has gathered a wealth of experience and know-how. We would like to share our knowledge and give young people the chance to start their career with us.”

cotedo became an official training company in 2015. Angela Burgmair came on board on 1 January 2016. She mainly worked for the Sales & Distribution Management team and over time, took over more and more responsible tasks, e.g. the order management for our German BMW customers. Carolin Junghans, leader of cotedo’s Sales & Distribution Management team, is happy: “During her apprenticeship, Ms Burgmair mainly supported our team. However, she also gained experience in other departments, which will surely come in handy during her time at university and for her future career.”

Training manager Christian Pruner chaffs: “This was not so bad for the first time. In fact, I see the good exam results as a commendation for all of us. I am looking forward to training more colleagues at cotedo in the future.”
