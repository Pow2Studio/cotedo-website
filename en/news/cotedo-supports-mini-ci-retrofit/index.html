New CI for MINI dealerships: cotedo is responsible for orders & supply for retrofit kits

11/14/2016

cotedo supports MINI dealerships with a CI retrofit campaign

MINI has grown up, and the CI of the premium car brand has been changed accordingly. Therefore an extensive retrofit campaign was launched for the furniture of all MINI dealerships in Germany.

cotedo was responsible for the order management and the supply coordination of the retrofit kits. Christian Pruner, deputy department leader Order Management, is happy: “We have registered and processed more than 200 individual orders for the changeover of the Colour and Material Element and the Consultation Satellites in the German MINI dealerships.

We are glad that our intensive commitment paid off and that we were able to successfully support the BMW Group during the implementation of the new MINI CI.”
