New Aftersales consultant

05/12/2017

Team Automotive Aftersales Consulting welcomes another new member.

In April 2017, Georgios Dimoulas joined our Consulting team. He can look back on years of experience in an international working environment.

After growing up in Germany, Georgios completed his university degree and subsequently worked as a vehicle development engineer and as a technical trainer. In recent years, he was Head of Aftersales for a large Audi importer in Greece. His knowledge and experience, paired with our profound know-how, will now help him tackle new projects and challenges.

We are happy to welcome another designated Aftersales expert in our team and wish him all the best for a successful start!
