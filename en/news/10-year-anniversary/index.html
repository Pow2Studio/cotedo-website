cotedo celebrates 10th anniversary: stylish party high above Munich's rooftops

Since the summer of 2017, cotedo can look back on ten years of successful business. This big birthday called for a proper party. More than 100 employees, customers, partners and associates celebrated in a relaxed atmosphere and enjoyed the setting: an elegant rooftop terrace. Everyone was stunned by the panoramic view all over Munich and beyond, all the way to the Alps.

“We are proud to have made a mark as much sought-after consultants and service providers“, said cotedo’s director Stefan Gatt during his welcome speech. “Whether we support car dealerships in optimising their aftersales processes and equipment or help large companies and manufacturers with their rollouts and sales activities – we have made an excellent name for ourselves as a competent and independent partner, for both customers and suppliers.“ Accordingly, the guest list was peppered with well-known names from the automobile and workshop industries.

In the mild evening sun, customers and suppliers, business partners, members of staff and other associates engaged in pleasant conversation and celebrated a laid-back party far into the night.

We would like to thank all the guests for attending, and for their congratulations and presents. We are looking forward to our next missions and projects together!
