Expert knowledge for car dealerships!

cotedo is a consulting and project management company in Munich. For more than ten years, we have been active in the automotive aftersales industry. We know: an excellent, professional realisation is the basis for economic success. This how we create tailor-made, optimal solutions for international enterprises and medium-sized companies.

We are happy to support you in structuring and realising your projects, and provide maximum performance in four competencies!

Capabilities

Charging infrastructure and electric mobility
The best possible solution from one source: charging infrastructure for car dealerships – bespoke & independent from manufacturers and products.
Implementation of standards and auditing
OEM standards guarantee comparable standards in both sales and aftersales. We make sure they are realized and accepted by the markets.
Equipment consulting
Functional equipment is vital for profitable business. We know your demands and provide advice – 100 % independently from manufacturers and products.
Aftersales structures and potentials
Industrial Aftersales: our approach is aimed at workspaces equipped according to tasks (not people), shorter paths and radically reduced process costs.
Building and planning car dealerships
We plan and realize new buildings and refurbishments for car dealerships. Investors and architects benefit from our profound, specific know-how. 
Q&A Manager app
Universal tool for audits, surveys and status tracking: intuitive, offline, no media discontinuities. Provides for comprehensive evaluation options.
Rollout management
Advanced project management: synchronal rollout of a multitude of homogeneous projects. Strict time and risk management are indispensable.
Procurement and purchasing 
The right equipment at the right place at the right time. We provide support by comparing offers, renegotiating, and managing your logistics.
Order management
We optimize your distribution processes and manage your orders. A feasible, economic and flexible addition to your team.
Interface management, PMO
Project management competence combined with modern tools. We create acceptance and competence by all persons affected.
Process design, change management
We define structured processes to reach your aims. Where required, we provide additional support by taking over the operational implementation.
Car dealerships are our passion
For years, we have been concentrating on car dealerships and workshops. Therefore we are familiar with all their requirements and demands.
